About the
Society

The Research & Debate Society (RDS) at Islamia College University Peshawar is a student-led intellectual society dedicated to fostering rigorous academic discourse, critical thinking, and evidence-based debate on the issues that shape our world.

"Knowledge as a Human Right"
Our founding principle
Our Purpose
What We Stand For
✦
Research & Publishing

We publish student-written articles, opinion pieces, and research papers across history, politics, science, and social issues — giving young researchers a credible platform for their ideas.

◈
Critical Debate

We host structured debate sessions that challenge students to defend positions with evidence and logic — building the intellectual confidence to engage on complex topics.

◉
Open Knowledge

All our published content is freely accessible. We believe quality research and analysis shouldn't be locked behind paywalls — especially for students and communities with limited access.
