Submit Work

Publish with RDS

We welcome submissions from students in Peshawar and the broader academic community. We publish four types of writing: general Articles, Opinion pieces, peer-reviewed Research, and Review Articles.

If you would like to submit a piece for consideration, please email us with your article as a Word document attachment, along with a short author bio (two to three sentences) and the category you believe your work fits best.

We review all submissions and respond within a reasonable timeframe. Work should be original, properly cited, and not under consideration elsewhere at the time of submission.
