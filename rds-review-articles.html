01
What is a Review Article?

A review article surveys and critically evaluates existing published work on a particular topic — identifying key debates, schools of thought, methodological strengths and weaknesses, and open questions the field has yet to resolve.

02
What We're Looking For

We welcome literature reviews, book reviews, and historiographical essays. Your review should have a clear argument about the state of knowledge on your topic — not just a summary of what others have said.

03
Submission Guidelines

Reviews should typically be 1,500–4,000 words. Please include a list of works reviewed or cited. All fields — history, politics, science, literature, economics — are welcome. Submit via the contact page.
